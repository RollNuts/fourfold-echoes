## Style Direction

Push characters and creatures more pop/deformed:

- 2.8 to 3.2 heads tall
- large readable heads and hands
- compact torsos
- chunky boots
- bright separated color regions
- simple role props
- low/mid-poly modelable shapes

Avoid:

- ultra-real modeling targets
- detailed MMO armor density
- copied famous silhouettes
- average fantasy clone characters
- small filigree that only works in a painting

## Part-Mining Verdict

- Hero sheet: low for whole characters, medium for hair, scarves, shields,
  goggles, hoods, antlers, chef silhouette, oversized tool props.
- Enemy/hazard sheet: high for crystal clusters, turret shapes, trap pit, shell
  tanks, rolling hazards, bomb cores, totems, elemental body chunks.
- Boss sheet: medium-high for massing, weak points, phase parts, and arena-scale
  silhouettes.
- NPC sheet: medium for service props and role silhouettes; low for final local
  identity as-is.
