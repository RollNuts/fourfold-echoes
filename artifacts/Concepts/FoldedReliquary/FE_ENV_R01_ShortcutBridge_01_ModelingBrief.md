## Asset Role

Route Surface / R01 shortcut bridge.

This asset defines the floor and route language for the first production pass.
It must not look like a rectangular tile bridge.

## Gameplay Read

From top-down/high three-quarter camera, the player should read:

- this is an opened route
- it connects two path sockets
- it belongs to the same folded reliquary product line as pedestals and rewards
- one side is weathered/broken, but the bridge is functional

## Required Construction Tokens

- asymmetrical folded shell-stone leaves
- central teal enamel signal groove
- brass hinge spine under or beside the groove
- two visible connection sockets
- thick beveled lips
- one localized broken/chipped cluster
- dark teal underside support mass
- small R01 weathering tabs, not standalone plants

## Forbidden Shapes

- rectangular slab bridge
- square tile grid
- medieval cobblestone
- generic fantasy floor mosaic
- scattered leaf/flower decoration
- crystal, chest, sword, shield, wand, or mascot forms
- symmetrical flower medallion as the main silhouette

## Material Palette

- body: warm ivory weathered shell-stone
- underside: dark teal-black support material
- signal: oxidized teal enamel
- hardware: aged brass
- locked/damaged accent: dull red wax only if needed
- weathering: sparse moss stains in cracks, below 10% of visible area

## Modeling Targets

- mid-poly LOD0 with broad bevels
- LOD1 preserves outer leaf silhouette and signal groove
- LOD2 preserves connection sockets and underside silhouette
- pivot at route connection center
- length reads as a short bridge module, not a room floor
- up to four role materials for this P0 reference asset: body, underside,
  hinge/accent, and signal

## Screenshot Acceptance

Reject the model if the gameplay render reads as:

- a generic stone bridge
- a square modular tile
- a decorative fantasy floor
- a natural moss/plant prop

Accept only if the silhouette still reads as folded shell-stone plates with a
functional signal path.
