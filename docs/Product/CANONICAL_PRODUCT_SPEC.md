## Canonical Direction

FOURFOLD ECHOES is a Steam-first, buy-to-own, single-player top-down classic
action-adventure.

The MVP is capped at:

- 1 hub
- 3 handcrafted regions
- 4 bosses
- 1 exploration tool

The game is not an open world. The game does not use Echo Phase world-state
switching, inventory, crafting, quest logs, social systems, multiplayer, live
services, or base-building loops in MVP.

## Production Gate

Do not expand production content until the vertical slice proves:

- readable top-down movement and combat
- one exploration tool that changes room navigation or reveals a solution
- one hub and one region
- two normal enemies, one miniboss, and one boss
- one shortcut opening
- two gimmick rooms
- two relic rewards
- minimal UI
- two music tracks
- minimum SFX set
- local save/load
- stylized 3D visuals beyond gray box
- audio that is not placeholder

## Historical Directions

Older documents and prototypes may mention Gate A, ProductReviewSandbox, Echo
Phase, extraction, co-op-ready systems, hack-and-slash loot, or compact
open-world structure. Those are historical only. They stay rejected unless the
entire D-020 canon is explicitly superseded across product, production, art,
audio, QA, and validation documents.
