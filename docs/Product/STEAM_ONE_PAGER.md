# Steam One-Pager

Status: D-020 draft.

## Title

FOURFOLD ECHOES

## Tagline

A compact top-down fantasy adventure built around one mysterious exploration
tool.

## Short Description

Explore three handcrafted regions from a small hub, use one exploration tool to
open paths and secrets, and defeat four readable bosses in a focused
single-player action-adventure.

## Long Description Draft

FOURFOLD ECHOES is a single-player top-down action-adventure for players who
want a compact, handcrafted fantasy game without open-world sprawl or live
service systems.

The rules stay small: move, fight, dodge, use one exploration tool, open
shortcuts, find relic rewards, and defeat bosses. The depth comes from room
layout, enemy placement, boss tells, and learning how the same tool solves new
problems.

## Feature Bullets

- Explore one hub and three compact fantasy regions.
- Master one exploration tool across rooms, shortcuts, secrets, and boss
  openings.
- Fight readable enemies and four bosses built for top-down action.
- Find relic rewards without inventory or crafting bloat.
- Play a complete buy-to-play adventure with no account, server, or live-service
  dependency.
