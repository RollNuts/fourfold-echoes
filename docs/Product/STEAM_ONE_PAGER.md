## Title

FOURFOLD ECHOES

## Positioning

Steam-first, buy-to-play, single-player, compact top-down action-adventure.

## Tagline

A compact top-down fantasy adventure built around one mysterious exploration

## Short Description

Explore compact fantasy regions from a small hub, use one exploration tool to
open paths and hidden routes, and defeat readable bosses in a focused
single-player action-adventure.

## 短文紹介3案

1. Explore a compact top-down fantasy adventure where one mysterious tool opens
   paths, reveals hidden routes, and creates boss openings.
2. Leave a small hub, cross three handcrafted regions, solve tool-based rooms,
   open shortcuts, and defeat four readable bosses.
3. A focused single-player action-adventure with one hub, one exploration tool,
   compact regions, and clear boss fights.

## 長文紹介

FOURFOLD ECHOES is a compact single-player top-down action-adventure for Steam.

From a small hub, enter up to three handcrafted fantasy regions, learn one
mysterious exploration tool, open shortcuts, find relic rewards, and defeat four
readable bosses. The adventure is built around a small rule set used repeatedly:
move, dodge, strike, read enemy tells, and use the same tool to solve rooms,
reveal routes, and create brief boss openings.

The store page should show the actual game within the first 10 seconds of video:
top-down movement, the hero, an enemy or boss threat, a tool target, and a route
or opening changing on screen. Do not promise features outside implemented work
or the D-020 cap.

## Feature Bullets

- Explore one small hub and up to three compact fantasy regions.
- Master one exploration tool across rooms, shortcuts, hidden rewards, and boss
  openings.
- Fight readable enemies and up to four bosses built for top-down action.
- Find relic rewards without adding heavy menu management.
- Play a complete buy-to-play adventure with local progression.
