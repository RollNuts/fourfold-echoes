# Market Target

Status: D-020 draft.

## Positioning

FOURFOLD ECHOES should target the premium-indie compact action-adventure lane:
focused, readable, controller-friendly, stylized, and complete.

The target pitch is not "infinite freedom" or "open world". It is:

> one hub, three compact regions, one exploration tool, four bosses, and a
> finished adventure without filler systems.

## Buyer Promise

- clear top-down action
- a memorable exploration tool
- room-level secrets and shortcuts
- readable bosses
- stylized fantasy look
- music and SFX that support play
- no live-service burden

## Market Validation Assets

- 8 real gameplay screenshots
- 45-second trailer
- 75-second trailer
- 20-30 minute demo
- Steam Deck-oriented QA note
- Japanese and English store text

## Avoided Promises

- open world
- endless builds
- crafting economy
- multiplayer
- live service
- AI-generated content claims
