# Intent Audit

Status: updated after D-020.

## Current Durable Intent

FOURFOLD ECHOES is now a compact single-player top-down classic
action-adventure:

- Steam-first.
- Buy-to-play.
- No live service.
- No open world.
- One hub.
- Three regions maximum.
- Four bosses maximum.
- One exploration tool.
- Stylized 3D.
- Audio and art are part of validation, not late polish.

## What Survived

- Unity as the shipping runtime path.
- Private commercial repo separate from public Veripsa dogfood.
- Steam-first, controller-first, local save discipline.
- Art/audio-first vertical-slice pressure.
- Veripsa/GitHub/Unity separation as a safe development structure.
- CLI/file-contract automation as stable evidence.

## What Was Superseded

| Previous Direction | Status | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gate A altar/gate proof | historical | useful technical harness, not product identity |
| ProductReviewSandbox composition | historical evidence | not a production slice |
| Compact open-world adventure | superseded | still too broad for current target |
| Echo Phase world-state hook | superseded | adds multiple systems; current design centers one tool |
| Extraction/run pressure | rejected | wrong product shape |
| Co-op-ready architecture as v1 promise | rejected | scope and QA cost too high |
| Hack-and-slash loot framing | rejected | inventory/build treadmill risk |

## Current Product Sentence

> I left a small hub, explored compact fantasy regions, mastered one exploration
> tool, opened shortcuts, found relic rewards, and defeated readable bosses.

Any future work that pulls the game toward open world, multiple tools,
inventory/crafting, live service, co-op, or placeholder market validation is
misaligned unless a newer accepted decision supersedes D-020.
