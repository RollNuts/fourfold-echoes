Status: D-020 planning document.

## 短文紹介3案

1. **Primary candidate, 137 chars:** A compact top-down fantasy action-adventure built around one mysterious
   exploration tool, readable combat, and handcrafted boss rooms.
2. **Alternate, 128 chars:** Explore three stylized regions, open shortcuts, solve tool-based rooms, and
   defeat four bosses in a focused single-player adventure.
3. **Alternate, 116 chars:** A classic action-adventure for Steam: one hub, three regions, one exploration
   tool, four bosses, and no filler systems.

## 長文紹介

FOURFOLD ECHOES is a compact single-player top-down action-adventure.

Leave a small hub, explore three handcrafted fantasy regions, master one
mysterious exploration tool, open shortcuts, claim relic rewards, and defeat
four readable bosses. The game is designed around a small rule set used well:
clear rooms, visible dangers, satisfying combat, and secrets that can be
understood from what you see on screen.

No bloated map sprawl, no live service, no crafting treadmill. The goal is a
focused premium adventure that feels good to play and reads clearly in motion.
