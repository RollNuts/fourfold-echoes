# Music Brief

Status: D-020 draft.

## Role

Music must make a compact top-down adventure feel memorable without hiding
combat or tool feedback.

## Required Cues

| Cue | Role | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `BGM_Hub` | safety, return, central motif | needed |
| `BGM_Region01` | first exploration area | needed for slice unless combined with hub by design |
| `BGM_Region02` | pressure region | planned after slice |
| `BGM_Region03` | late mystery region | planned after slice |
| `BGM_NormalCombat` | optional combat loop/layer | defer unless combat needs it |
| `BGM_Boss` | first boss fight and boss language | needed |
| `BGM_DiscoveryReward` | discovery and relic reward stinger | needed |

## Direction

- one short memorable motif
- warm hub version
- more driven exploration version if budget allows
- boss loop with clear downbeats for attack tells
- discovery/reward stingers short enough not to interrupt control
- no complex stem system for MVP
- no region track is accepted until enemy tells and tool cues remain audible

## Acceptance

The vertical slice is not complete until hub/Region 01 music, boss music,
tool SFX, combat SFX, reward/discovery stinger, and UI basics are audible and
mixed in gameplay context.
