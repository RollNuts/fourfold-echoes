# AI Usage Policy

AI may assist:

- ideation
- composition thumbnails
- blockout models
- material variations
- naming and metadata drafts
- temporary test assets

AI may not be treated as final source without:

- tool/service name
- terms snapshot date
- commercial-use confirmation
- prompt summary
- human edit record
- style/trademark/likeness review
- asset registry entry

Do not prompt for or imitate living artists, protected characters, or specific contemporary game assets.
