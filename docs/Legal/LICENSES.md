## License Policy

- Use commercial-license-only assets.
- "Free" is not enough; commercial use, modification, redistribution-in-game,
  and attribution requirements must be known.
- Marketplace receipts or private account data must not be committed.
- Public repo paths, tokens, keys, PEM files, and private package credentials
  must not be committed.
- AI-generated assets require tool terms, prompt summary, human edit record,
  review date, and final human quality approval.
- Shipped screenshots, store copy, audio, UI, and models must match registered
  assets.
