## Current Direction

FOURFOLD ECHOES uses stylized 3D for a compact top-down action-adventure.
The game must read clearly from gameplay distance. The one exploration tool,
enemy tells, room routes, shortcuts, rewards, and bosses are the primary visual
jobs.

Old multi-state or broad-world visual guidance is historical unless it is
reframed to support the current one-tool compact MVP.

## Production Use

Use `COMPACT_ACTION_ART_DIRECTION.md` as the production checklist for:

- Art pillars.
- Prohibited visual directions.
- Asset budgets for materials, textures, VFX, and animation density.
- Naming rules for props, terrain, enemies, UI, and VFX.
- Hub and three-region look development.
- Minimum quality bars for an enemy, room, gimmick pedestal, and chest.
- The approved art pass order from gray blockout to final polish.
- What may be cut versus what must remain visible.

Any new art guidance must preserve D-020: single-player only, Steam-first,
premium purchase, top-down classic action-adventure, compact scope, no open
world, one exploration tool, and MVP cap of one hub, three regions, and four
bosses.
