## Current Direction

FOURFOLD ECHOES uses stylized 3D for a compact top-down action-adventure.
The game must read clearly from gameplay distance. The one exploration tool,
enemy tells, room routes, shortcuts, rewards, and bosses are the primary visual
jobs.

All production-intent 3D models must belong to the same Folded Reliquary
product line. Regional motifs are material/weathering variants, not separate
genres or loose fantasy asset themes.

Production-intent 3D models now require a concept image first. Text-only
generation, old test meshes, and generic RPG tropes are not sufficient modeling
sources.

Old multi-state or broad-world visual guidance is historical unless it is
reframed to support the current one-tool compact MVP.
